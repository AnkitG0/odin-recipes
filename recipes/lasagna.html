Lasagna


This recipe describes the perfect way to make Lasagna using cheap vegetables



Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 bunch fresh spinach
  • 1 (8 ounch) container ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten


Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  3. Cook lasagna in the boiling water.
  4. Steam spinach for 2 to 6 minutes
  5. Mix spinach, ricotta cheese, mozzarella, egg, nutmeg, basil, salt, and black peter in a bowl untill thoroughly combined
